About Us

The Problem

We’re all guilty of mistakenly throwing away trash in a recycling bin or tossing a paper cup in a disposal meant for plastic. According to Waste Management, an American trash collecting company, the average recycling contamination rate — or disposal of trash or recyclables in the wrong recycling bin — is 25%, meaning 1 in 4 items thrown in a recycling bin isn’t recyclable. (epa.gov) Additionally, only 32% of all people even make an effort to recycle. That's less than 1/3 of the population. By throwing away "trash" that could be reused, we are not only wasting valuable resources, but also requireing more materials to be used to create various products. As a result, global warming and climate destructions are at an all time high.

The Solution

This is why we created EnviroHelp. EnviroHelp is a web app which educates people about recycling and how everyone can do their share to keep the planet clean. By using a trained and tested achine learning model, users are able to input a picture of the item they want to dispose of, and upload them to our site. EnviroHelp returns whether the image is glass, plastic, trash, metal, or paper, and the proper steps to get rid of that item, without harming the enviroment.
